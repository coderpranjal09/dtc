About Delhi Traffic

Delhi's traffic is known for its bustling roads and diverse vehicular movement. The Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) plays a crucial role in managing public transport and ensuring that buses run smoothly across the city. With an extensive network of routes, DTC buses help reduce congestion on the roads and provide an affordable mode of transportation for millions of Delhiites.

Delhi Bus Schedules & Timings

DTC buses operate on a fixed schedule, ensuring timely service across various parts of Delhi and the NCR region. The buses are well-maintained and run throughout the day, with peak hours seeing an increased frequency to accommodate the heavy passenger load.
